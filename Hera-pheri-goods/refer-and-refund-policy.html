Refer & Earn and Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2025

How it works?

Herapheri Goods's Refer & Earn Program is an exclusive reward system that gives you money when you invite your friends or contacts to use Herapheri Goods! 💰

Referral Rewards 🎁

Free Listing Referral Bonus

  • If someone creates a FREE listing through your referral, you’ll earn 5 coins/user.

Paid Listing Referral Bonus

  • If someone registers a vehicle and upgrades to a paid listing through your referral, you'll receive a bonus based on the plans mentioned below.
  • ₹59/month plan → 10 coins referral reward
  • ₹159/3 month plan → 30 coins referral reward
  • ₹299/6 month plan → 60 coins referral reward
  • ₹499/year plan → 120 coins referral reward

Coins Expiry System ⏳

  • Stay active! If no new referrals are made within 3 months, your coin balance will begin decreasing by 2 coins each day.
  • If you make a new referral or a paid transaction, your 3-month countdown will reset.

💵 Minimum Withdrawal

  • Withdrawal is only allowed when your balance reaches 500 coins.

Step 2: Encourage Paid Listing

✅ Paid Listing Benefits

  • Referral & Earn Program is exclusively available to Premium Vehicle Owners Only.
  • Your vehicle will be shown at the top of the list.
  • Your vehicle will attract more customers.
  • Premium account holders can register up to 5 vehicles.
  • Premium badge on profile.
  • Earn real money by sharing your referral link
  • Premium owners can get a chance to grab more customers
  • Get you custom profile url.

✅ Paid Listing Premium Bonus

  • Unlock earning opportunities by upgrading to a Premium Vehicle listing.
  • Referral earnings will only be earned on paid listings.

Refund Policy

  • Non-Refundable: Once a paid listing is approved and live on the website, it is non-refundable.
  • In case of double payment or failed transaction, the amount will be refunded within 7-10 working days after verification.
  • If the listing is not approved due to website policies, the full refund will be processed.
  • If you face any issues regarding payments or refunds, please contact our support team at info@herapherigoods.in within 24 hours of the transaction.

Changes to Refer & Earn Policy

We may update this Refer & Earn Policy from time to time without any prior notice. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated Effective Date.
