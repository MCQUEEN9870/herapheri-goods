Frequently asked questions

How does Herapheri Goods work?

Herapheri Goods is a platform that connects transport vehicle owners directly with customers. You can search for available vehicles in your area based on your requirements, view vehicle details and contact information, and connect directly with the vehicle owner without any middlemen or extra charges.

What types of vehicles are available on your platform?

We offer a wide range of transport vehicles including small commercial vehicles (like Tata Ace, Auto Loaders, E-Rickshaws), medium commercial vehicles (like Eicher Canter, Bolero Pickup), heavy commercial vehicles (Container Trucks, Flatbed Trucks), special utility vehicles (JCB, Cranes, Dumpers), refrigerated vehicles, and emergency vehicles like ambulances and tow trucks.

Is there any registration fee to use your services?

No, there is no registration fee for customers looking to hire vehicles. For vehicle owners, basic registration is free. We operate on a transparent model where you only pay for the services you use.

How do I find a vehicle for my transport needs?

Simply visit our "Find Vehicles" page, enter your location and select the type of vehicle you need. Our system will show you available vehicles in your area. You can then view details and directly contact the vehicle owner to discuss your requirements and pricing.

Is my personal information safe with Herapheri Goods?

Yes, we take data privacy very seriously. Your personal information is protected as per our Privacy Policy. We use industry-standard security measures to ensure your data remains confidential and is only used for the purposes you've consented to.

Do you verify the vehicle owners on your platform?

We perform basic verification of vehicle owners by checking their identity and vehicle documents. However, we strongly advise users to exercise their own judgment when dealing with vehicle owners. We are primarily an information platform connecting vehicle owners with customers.

Are you responsible for any fraud or misconduct by vehicle owners?

Herapheri Goods is not responsible for the conduct or services provided by vehicle owners listed on our platform. We only provide a platform to connect vehicle owners with customers. We encourage users to report any misconduct so we can take appropriate action, but the transaction and service agreement is solely between you and the vehicle owner.

What transport services do you provide?

We provide a platform for various transport services including goods transportation, vehicle rental, packers and movers services, construction equipment rental (JCB, cranes), refrigerated transport for perishable goods, emergency vehicle services (ambulance, tow trucks), and specialized transport solutions for various industries.

Do you offer interstate transport services?

Yes, many vehicle owners on our platform offer interstate transport services. You can filter your search by location and distance to find vehicles available for long-distance transport. The terms, pricing, and availability for interstate services are determined by the individual vehicle owners.

Can I book specialized vehicles like refrigerated trucks or construction equipment?

Yes, our platform includes specialized vehicles such as refrigerated trucks for transporting perishable goods, construction equipment like JCBs and cranes, and other utility vehicles. You can filter your search by vehicle type to find these specialized options in your area.

Do you provide packers and movers services?

Yes, we have packers and movers listed on our platform. These service providers offer complete relocation solutions including packing, loading, transportation, unloading, and unpacking. You can contact them directly through our platform to discuss your specific requirements and pricing.

Can I hire vehicles for regular/daily transport needs?

Yes, many vehicle owners on our platform offer their vehicles for regular or daily hire. You can discuss the terms, schedule, and pricing directly with the vehicle owner. This is particularly useful for businesses with regular transport requirements.

Do you provide emergency transport services?

Yes, we have ambulances and emergency response vehicles listed on our platform. These can be contacted directly in case of emergencies. We recommend saving relevant contact information beforehand for quicker access during emergencies.

What is the Driver Dashboard?

The Driver Dashboard is a dedicated portal for vehicle owners registered on our platform. It allows you to manage your profile, update vehicle information, track customer inquiries, manage bookings, view analytics, and access various tools to help grow your transport business.

How do I access my Driver Dashboard?

After registering your vehicle, you can access your Driver Dashboard by logging in with your credentials. Once logged in, you'll be automatically redirected to your dashboard. You can also click on the profile icon in the navigation bar to access your dashboard.

How can I update my vehicle information or availability?

You can update your vehicle information and availability status through your Driver Dashboard. Navigate to the "My Vehicles" section, select the vehicle you want to update, and make the necessary changes. Don't forget to save your changes before exiting.

Can I list multiple vehicles under one account?

Yes, you can list multiple vehicles under a single account. In your Driver Dashboard, go to the "My Vehicles" section and click on "Add New Vehicle." You'll need to provide details and documentation for each vehicle separately.

How do I respond to customer inquiries?

Customer inquiries will appear in the "Inquiries" section of your Driver Dashboard. You can view the details of each inquiry and respond directly through the platform. Prompt responses increase your chances of securing bookings.

How can I improve my visibility on the platform?

To improve your visibility: 1) Keep your profile and vehicle information complete and up-to-date, 2) Upload clear, high-quality photos of your vehicle, 3) Respond promptly to inquiries, 4) Maintain good ratings and reviews, and 5) Consider our premium listing options for enhanced visibility.
